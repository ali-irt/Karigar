Privacy Policy

At Karigar, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and address provided during registration or booking.
  • Service Usage: Details of services booked, Karigar interactions, and preferences.
  • Payment Data: Secure payment details, transaction IDs, and billing information.
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, cookies, and analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Data:

  • To provide and improve our services efficiently.
  • To communicate important updates, confirmations, and notifications.
  • To personalize user experience and recommend services.
  • To maintain platform security and prevent fraud.

3. Sharing of Information:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Verified Karigars for completing service requests.
  • Service providers assisting with payment processing or analytics.
  • Law enforcement agencies when required by law.

4. Cookies and Tracking:

We use cookies and analytics to improve website and app performance. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. User Rights:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

6. Security Measures:

We employ industry-standard measures including encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Contact Us:

For any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact our support team at support@karigar.pk.
